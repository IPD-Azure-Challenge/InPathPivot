About InPath Detroit

A new era of workforce development that will change virtual education and innovation.

Welcome to InPath Detroit, we focus on online software development training, empowering at-risk populations by offering technology and soft skills to promote long-term success. 

Our workforce development program provides each of our participating students with the competencies they need in Technical, Vocational Training or Skilled Trade Partnerships. We focus both on what student needs to know to be competitive now, and on how they can continue to grow and develop their skills over time. 

Our online workforce development opportunities and partnerships will strengthen the talent pipeline and provide opportunities to at-risk populations by providing coursework both on-demand and via remote classroom and online learning.
